About Ozuchi village

10 houses, 1 people and 1 cat

 Ozuchi is a marginal settlement in Kaga-Onsen area, Ishikawa prefecture JAPAN, with only 10 houses, 1 villager and 1 cat.
 While facing a crisis of disapearance because of its severe depopulation, Ozuchi is attracting many people from all over the world including other regions of Japan.
 This is a place you can enjoy the traditional Japanese life style and scenery, which you cannot experience in big cities such as Tokyo, Kyoto or Osaka etc.
